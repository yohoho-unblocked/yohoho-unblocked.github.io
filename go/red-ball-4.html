Embark on a thrilling adventure with "Red Ball 4," a captivating browser game that will test your platforming skills and reflexes. Join Red Ball on a quest to save the world from evil squares and navigate through challenging levels filled with traps and puzzles.

How to Play

Move: Use ← → / A D keys to navigate Red Ball through the treacherous terrain.

Jump: Press ↑ / W / ␣ Space / ↵ Enter to make Red Ball leap over obstacles and hazards.
