Delve into the captivating world of "Idle Ants," a browser-based game that puts you in charge of a bustling ant colony. With its addictive gameplay and strategic depth, Idle Ants offers an engaging experience for players looking to build and manage their own insect empire.

How to Play Idle Ants

Send your ants to disassemble objects and carry the pieces back home. Boost speed for faster colony work, recruit new ants for increased workforce, and enhance strength for carrying larger pieces.
